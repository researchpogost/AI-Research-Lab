Just after Test

1:
Soft iron is put in hot coals, so it glows.  
Slip it out with tongs and lay it on an anvil.  
Shape it by striking and rolling, so it turns round and flat.  
Slowly tap and turn until both tips join, forming a ring.

2:
Smithing starts with hot coal till iron glows. 
Strong blows upon an anvil fashion a round ring. 
Smooth arcs form as sparking tips join. 
Such small work sits within a glass box.
